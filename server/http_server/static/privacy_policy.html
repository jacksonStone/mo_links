Privacy Policy for Mo Links Chrome Extension

Effective Date: July 24, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Mo Links Chrome extension ("we," "our," or "the Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

2. Information Collection

The Mo Links Chrome extension collects minimal data about users. Specifically:

  • The Extension creates records of associated links to short links if a user choses to do this within the popup window.
  • The Extension expands "mo/" to "https://www.molinks.me/" within the Chrome browser.

No other personal information or user-generated content is collected or stored by the Extension.

3. Use of Information

The associated links data is used solely for the purpose of providing the link shortening and expansion service within your organization. This data allows the Extension to correctly expand short links to their full URLs.

4. Data Storage

If a user choses to create records of associated links to short links in the popup window, the data is stored securely within the the molinks.me database to enable the user to share links within their organization.

5. Data Sharing

The Extension does not share the associated links data with any third parties. The data remains within your organization's use of the Extension.

6. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of the Extension's code, functionality, and the associated links data stored locally. However, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their associated links data
  • Request deletion of their data
  • Use the Extension without creating any associated links

8. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or process data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this Privacy Policy.

10. Third-Party Services

The Extension redirects users to https://www.molinks.me/. Please note that this website is not covered by this Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of https://www.molinks.me/ for information on their data practices.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Mo Links Chrome extension, please contact us at [Your Contact Information].

12. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling any personal data we collect in accordance with these laws.

By using the Mo Links Chrome extension, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and use of associated links data as described herein.
